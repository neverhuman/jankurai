# Code Of Conduct

Jankurai contributors are expected to keep project spaces professional, specific, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in issues, pull requests, reviews, and discussions.
- Critique code, claims, tests, and documentation directly without attacking people.
- Assume public project spaces are read by users with different backgrounds and experience levels.
- Keep security reports private until maintainers coordinate disclosure.
- Respect maintainer decisions about scope, risk, and release timing.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated off-topic, disruptive, or bad-faith participation.
- Publicly disclosing vulnerabilities before maintainers have had a reasonable chance to respond.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, restrict participation, or block accounts when behavior harms the project or its contributors. Enforcement should be proportionate to the impact and documented when practical.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to a maintainer through GitHub. For security issues, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead.
