\subsection{Vibe-Coding Source Coverage}

Jankurai 0.8.0 maps every parsed row in the vibe-coding corpus into
\texttt{agent/vibe-coverage.toml}. Coverage is
detector-backed only when a narrowed row has deterministic rule coverage plus
proof/report evidence; partial when Jankurai has a reviewed control family but
still needs issue-specific fixture, CI, or governance receipt evidence. The full
per-row registry is supplemental source material in
\texttt{agent/vibe-coverage.toml}; generated machine-readable outputs are
emitted under \texttt{target/jankurai/} by the documented coverage command.
